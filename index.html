About Me

Antonio "Tony" Cuffia, is currently a manager at the five-star rated Jiffy Lube in Reisterstown, Maryland. He has 10+ years of automotive field training and seven years of management experience. Currently studying web-coding from George Washington University and marketing at the Community College of Baltimore County Catonsville, Tony has set the bar extremely high for himself.In 2015, Tony and some of his colleagues established an internet following due to many social media savvy techniques. Their website www.LoveTheCulture.com has gathered interest from music industry veterans such as Kevin Liles,singer Mario, and popular Baltimore comedian Jess Hilarious. With the success of this site Tony plans to dig deeper into the world of coding and web design with plans to launch more websites and buisnesses. Tony is also a self-taught film director and editor with many popular videos gaining attention nationwide.
